# Tableau-project-on-Superstore-Sales-Analysis
This Tableau project analyzes Superstore sales across the U.S., focusing on trends, profit drivers, and state-wise performance. Key insights support opening a distribution center near Philadelphia to improve logistics and profitability in high-sales but low-margin areas like Pennsylvania.

**Title:** Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard 

**Tools Used:** Tableau **Short Description:** 

**Problem:** The business needed better visibility into sales trends, profit drivers, and regional performance across the U.S., especially in areas with high sales but low profitability. 

**What I Did:** Analyzed Superstore sales data in Tableau, creating interactive dashboards to track sales patterns, profit margins, and state-level performance, focused on identifying inefficiencies and uncovering actionable insights through data visualization. 

**Key Insights:** Discovered that states like Pennsylvania had high sales but low profit margins due to logistical inefficiencies. Recommended opening a distribution center near Philadelphia to optimize delivery costs and improve overall profitability.
